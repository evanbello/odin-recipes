Lasagna Recipe

Description

These sweet heat wings will have you wishing it only took 5 minutes to make. A crispy cruch, tender meat, and tasty sauce is all that encompasses these delicious finger foods. Read on to find out how YOU can make this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegetable or Canola Oil
  • Ketchup
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Spray a 13x9-inch or larger casserole dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Cut the chicken wings into 3 sections; discard the wing tip section. Coat with salt and pepper.
  3. Combine the remaining ingredients in a medium bowl until well mixed.
  4. Place the wings in a shallow casserole, pour the sauce over and bake until the chicken is well done and the sauce is caramelized, about 1 hour.
