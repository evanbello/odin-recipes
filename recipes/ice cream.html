Ice Cream Recipe

Description

This sweet butr savory ice cream will have you wishing it only took 5 minutes to make, instead of a whopping 2 hours! It will be well worth it though! Read on to find out how YOU can make this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Ice
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Food Coloring

Steps

  1. First, mix everything together
  2. Second, but everything into a blender
  3. Third, blend it until it becomes a gooey mess
  4. Lastly, throw it in the freezer for 2 hours at negative whatever degrees farenheit your freezer is currently at.
