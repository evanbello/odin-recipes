Lasagna Recipe

Description

This ooey gooey lasagna will have you wishing it only took 5 minutes to make, instead of a whopping 2 hours! It will be well worth it though! Read on to find out how YOU can make this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • More Sauce

Steps

  1. First, lay out the pasta
  2. Second, cover it in the ingredients
  3. Third, stack it up repeating the covering ad infinitum, or until it's as tall as you would like it
  4. Lastly, throw it in the oven for 2 hours at 400 degrees farenheit.
